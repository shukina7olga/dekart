Дизайн спальной комнаты в классическом стиле

Make has designed the interiors for 10 Park Drive, a 42-storey tower plus adjoining mid-rise building in Canary Wharf, designed by Stanton Williams. The development – the first residential scheme in a major masterplan that includes 3,200 homes – is set on a prime waterfront location and contains 345 apartments, all with striking views.

Our aim was to capture the development’s grand scale and form, and integrate it down to the smallest detail. The building is expressed as a solid block, with carvedout recesses articulated by fine metal detailing. We took this concept and visually echoed it throughout the interiors, from macro to micro details.
